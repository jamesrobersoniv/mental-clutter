While sun showered laundered sheets,<br>
My mother whispered, “move mountains.” <br>

I tried, gave up, and flew over instead. <br>
Same shit, different toilets I learned. <br>

My advice: pitch the tent, sow the seeds,<br>
Mend the fire, and dance to laughter. <br>

When snow melts, water runs.<br>
Plan accordingly.<br>
